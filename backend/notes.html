What are to_representation and to_internal_value? Serializers in the Django Rest Framework offer two methods: to_representation and to_internal_value. These techniques are used to translate between an object's internal representation and its serialised representation. The to_representation method is used to convert the internal representation of an object into its serialized representation. This is used when serializing an object to be sent over the wire. The to_internal_value method is used to convert the serialized representation of an object back into its internal representation. This is used when deserializing an object that has been received over the wire. ========================================================================================================================
